## Our Values

This is our promise to you that we are committed to the products we create and are proud to maintain strong core guiding principles as we continue on our path forwards.

<div class="strongheads" markdown="1">
Sustainable, Reliable, User-Driven
</div>


We're in this for the long haul. We aren't backing out of our game. The products we create are intended to last far into the future, powered by you the user and no one else. Whether you've been a long-time member of Wysc or a new user joining five years later, we want to still be here to welcome you. We don't plan on selling out to a company or exiting the industry anytime soon.

This requires a radical change in mindset when it comes to study Discords. Our bootstrapped processes mean we can provide you with excellent service and a welcome atmosphere **without ever asking for donations**. No Patreon, no monthly subscriptions, no freemium hoo-ha. **Just one clean, quality, free service for everyone to use. Forever.**

We want our server not to be driven by top-down policies, but for *you* as valued customers to help shape Wysc into what you'd like it to be. There should be no hard-and-fast rules against conversation topics (with the exception of [our Culture policy](../discord/culture)), and if you have any feature suggestions, we are always open in #support to hear your thoughts!
